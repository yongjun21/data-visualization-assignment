Trend of Rising Surface Temperature in Both Northern & Southern Hemisphere

This visualization shows global surface temperature change over time using data from GISS Surface Temperature Analysis (GISTEMP). I plotted mean surface temperatures against the year they are recorded from 1880 to 2015. The chart is broken down into temperature in the Northern hemisphere and temperature in the Southern hemisphere. On top of the scatter plot, I overlayed LOESS smoothed curves with 90% confidence interval band to further highlight the trend. We can see that surface temperature had risen almost 1.2Â°C in the last century. The rise is more pronounced in the Northern hemisphere.
